FAQ

Straight answers

Do we need to replace our software?

Almost never. We start with email, spreadsheets, CRM, accounting and job management tools, then connect them properly.

Is this just ChatGPT attached to our business?

No. Most value comes from well-built automation: enquiry capture, follow-up, CRM updates, reporting and reminders. AI is added only where it earns its place.

What does it cost?

The On-Site Automation Review is free while we launch. A Proof Sprint starts at $4,500. Core builds run from $15,000 to $35,000 depending on scope.

How quickly will we see results?

The Proof Sprint puts one automation live within weeks and measures the time it hands back.

What happens with our data?

Your data stays in your systems. We design for least access, human oversight and careful handling of sensitive information.
