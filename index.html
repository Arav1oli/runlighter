FAQ

Straight answers

Do we need to replace our software?

Almost never. We start with what you already run — email, spreadsheets, your CRM, accounting and job management tools — and connect them properly. Replacement is a last resort, not a starting point.

Is this just ChatGPT bolted onto our business?

No. Most of the value is well-built automation: enquiry capture, follow-up, CRM updates, reporting, reminders. We add AI only where it earns its place — drafting, summarising, sorting — always with a person across anything that matters.

What does it cost?

The On-Site Automation Review is free while we launch. A Proof Sprint starts at $4,500 and is credited toward a larger build. Core builds run $15,000 to $35,000 depending on scope. You'll always know the number before we start.

How quickly will we see results?

The Proof Sprint puts one automation live within weeks and measures the hours it hands back. No six-month roadmaps before anything works.

What happens with our data?

Your data stays in your systems. We work to Australian privacy guidance: least access, human oversight, and nothing sensitive ever goes into public AI tools.
